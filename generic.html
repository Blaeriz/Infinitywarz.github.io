Rules

[1] No stealing:
➥ You can only steal when you are at war with the enemy team, or as revenge.
[2] No hacking or abusing glitches:
➥ No use of hacked clients (or hacks in general, such as chest ESP).
➥ No use of exploits.
➥ No use of xray.
➥ In general try not to use anything that gives you an advantage over the other players!
[3] Do not be excessively toxic:
➥ Not much to explain for this one, just try not to be very toxic in chat.
[4] Do not use banned items and commands:
➥ List of banned items can be found in #banned-items.
➥ Banned commands are things such as doing /home or /tpa during a fight to get out.
[5] Do not test nukes and missiles:
➥ Don't test any explosives in the server world.
➥ Use singleplayer for testing.
[6] War:
➥ War is a very OP scenario, and a very brutal one as well. Starting a war with another team allows the use of nukes and missiles on each other's bases. For this reason, there must be a justified reason for war and a member of staff must confirm if you can start a war or not.
➥ If the war is not justified, both teams will be to carry on doing their own thing.
➥ If the war is justified, and it starts, the declaring team will be tagged first, then the 2nd team.
➥ Both teams do not have to agree on war, only one team can start it.
➥ Reason must be good enough, and valid.
[7] Raids:
➥ During a raid, you may not do any of the following:
- Take everything.
- Place C4 on, or around chests (it is okay if you have to toss one or two onto a door to get in).
- Purposely blow up / destroy chests.
- Mine into / out of the base.
- Building (stacking up with blocks) to get over the enemy base wall. Use the entrance to get out (or any side exits if there are any).
[8] Schematica:
➥ Schematica and printer are both allowed!
[9] Imprisonment:
➥ If you are imprisoned, you are to not use any commands such as /home, /tpa or /back.
➥ If you are imprisoned, you or or someone else must break you out (unless the team that has captured you, decides to let you go).
➥ You may only use /msg while imprisoned.
➥ If you have captured a member of staff, please note that they have duties! If they go out of your cell / area of where you have them captured, they will return when they are done with their staff duties.
➥ Other teams may break you out.
[10] Enemies, but not at war:
➥ You can use MCHeli to blow up their base.
➥ You must not use any missiles or nukes on the enemy base / in general on the enemy.
➥ You must only use MCHeli grenades.
- You must not blow up chests.
➥ You may blow up doors and windows.
➥ Must have a reason to blow up their base. Either as revenge or as part of the story-line.
➥ You must not blow up over 50% of their base unless at war.
[11] Bases:
➥ You must at least have 8x8 area, visible from land or sky.
➥ May not hide in a small bunker under ground for the entire map.
➥ Base must be within the 2,500 x 2,500 block border.
➥ Must have a visible entrance from land.
➥ Have a sign showing who's base this is.
[12] Looting bodies:
➥ You may only loot non-craftables (food, ores, etc.) if outside your base, or in wild.
➥ You may only loot the whole body if it's in your base (or in a 30 block radius from your land).
➥ If an event is going on, they may loot your entire body!
➥ If at war:
- Can loot entire body anywhere.
[13] Bedrock Border:
➥ You are not allowed to be on the bedrock border.
➥ You are not allowed to pass the bedrock border.
