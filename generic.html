Rules

[1] No hacking or abusing glitches:
➥ No use of hacked clients (or hacks in general, such as chest ESP).
➥ No use of exploits.
➥ No use of xray.
➥ No placing blocks like dirt, stone, cobble, grass, clay etc to try to get drops. That will be an instant ban for that game.
➥ In general try not to use anything that gives you an advantage over the other players!
[2] Do not be excessively toxic:
➥ Not much to explain for this one, just try not to be very toxic in chat.
[3] Schematica:
➥ Schematica and printer are both allowed!
[4] Base:
➥ Have a sign showing who's base this is.
➥ Your beacon must be in sight where you can reach it. [5] Time:
➥ Make sure you have time to play the gamemode!
➥ Quitting midgame makes you disqualified.
[6] BlackListed Mods:
- All sorts of minimaps.
- Chest ESP Mod.
- Tracers Mod.
- Freecam Mod.
- Mods that give you an advantage over other players.
